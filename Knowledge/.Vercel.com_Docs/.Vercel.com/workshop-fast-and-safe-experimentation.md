Build and deploy experiments live.
:::::

Vercel and Optimizely experts walk you through step by step how to
build, monitor, and analyze your own experiment. Learn best practices
for fast and safe experimentation, including managing feature flags on
Vercel, running effective A/B tests, and boosting ecommerce conversion
rates.

Topics covered:

- Introduction to experimentation
- Setting up Next.js with Optimizely
- Implementing & testing experiments
- Deployment, monitoring, & analysis

*This session is recommended for Next.js or React developers, architects
and solutions architects, and new Vercel or Optimizely users looking to
get a solid foundation.*
